How long does the UK Global Talent Visa process take?
The endorsement stage (proving you're talented) takes 1-3 months. After endorsement, the visa application takes another 3-4 weeks. Total timeline: 2-4 months if your profile is ready. If you need to build your profile first (media coverage, stronger portfolio), add 6-12 months.

NO! That's what makes this visa special. You don't need a job offer or company sponsorship. You can work for anyone, freelance, start a business, or switch jobs freely once in the UK. You just need to prove you're exceptionally talented in your field.

Yes! Your spouse and children can join you on the Global Talent Visa. They'll have the right to work and study in the UK. After 5 years (or 3 years if you're endorsed as exceptional talent), you can all apply for permanent residency.
